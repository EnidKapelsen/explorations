Badeschluss

Year: 2023 Medium: Short Film Context: Schmiede Hallein

Created during the Schmiede media arts festival in Hallein, Badeschluss emerged from spontaneous collaboration between participants. The work embraces the nostalgia and lightheartedness of summertime alongside the quiet melancholy of its passing.

In an era of accelerating AI-generated imagery, this handmade animation offers something deliberately slow - a kind of slow food for the heart.
